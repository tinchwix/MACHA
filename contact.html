Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What curriculum do you offer?

We offer the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) for Pre-primary, Primary and Junior Secondary School levels, ensuring a holistic education that nurtures every learner's potential.

What are the admission requirements?

For new admissions, please bring the learner's Birth Certificate, previous report forms (if transferring), and NEMIS number. An interview may be conducted for placement. Visit our Admissions page for more details.

How can I pay school fees?

School fees can be paid via M-PESA Paybill or directly to our bank account. We strive to keep our quality education affordable for the Maasai community. Please contact the accounts office for the latest fee structure.

Is boarding available?

Yes! We offer modern, safe, and separate boarding facilities for both boys and girls. Our dedicated matron and patron ensure a homely environment with spiritual guidance and nutritional support.
