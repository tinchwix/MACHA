Our Story

17 years of educational excellence, innovation, and transforming lives

Our Mission

MACHA is committed to providing a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens. We foster academic excellence, character development, and a lifelong love of learning in a nurturing, multicultural environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that shapes future leaders who make positive impacts in their communities and the world.

Why Choose MACHA?

We are dedicated to holistic development through four key pillars of excellence.

Engaging Minds

Fostering curiosity and critical thinking through innovative teaching methods and individualized attention.

Globally Focused

Preparing students to lead in an interconnected world by celebrating diversity and broad perspectives.

Preparing for Eternity

Anchoring academic success in a strong spiritual foundation that looks beyond this world.

Building Character

Instilling values of integrity, service, and responsibility as the true measures of success.

Our History

A legacy of excellence spanning over a decade

2010

Foundation

MACHA was founded with just 45 students and a vision to provide quality education rooted in strong values and academic rigor.

2015

International Expansion

Opened our doors to all Kenyan students, beginning our journey as a truly multicultural institution with students from 47 Counties.

2020

STEM Excellence Program

Launched our award-winning STEM program with state-of-the-art laboratories and partnerships with leading technology companies.

2025

Modern Campus Renovation

Completed a ksh 1.8 million campus modernization project, including new dormitories and innovation labs.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics, character, and service, challenging students to reach their full potential.

Integrity

We foster honesty, ethical behavior, and personal responsibility in all aspects of school life.

Compassion

We cultivate empathy, kindness, and service to others, building a caring and supportive community.

Global Citizenship

We prepare students to thrive in an interconnected world and make positive contributions to society.

Innovation

We embrace creativity, critical thinking, and adaptability to prepare students for tomorrow's challenges.

Diversity

We celebrate our multicultural community and learn from our differences to build understanding and respect.
