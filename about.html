Our History & Foundation

A story of hope, dedication, and transforming lives since 2009

Our Foundation

Window of Hope Kenya

The Window of Hope is a Non-Governmental Organization started in 2009 with an aim of supporting less fortunate children in society to have hope that there is a future for them. Founded by Pastor David Kikwai with support from well-wishers from Norway, the initiative began as a children's home to nurture children without parents (orphans) and those neglected by their parents.

Today, the program benefits more than 300 children directly, providing them with quality education, care, and hope for the future.

"The last thing one can do in life is give up hope. When you lose hope in life, it means you have nothing to live for. Your dreams, your visions, your ambitions, they all go up in flames."
- Pstr D. Kikwai
