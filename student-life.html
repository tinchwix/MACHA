Student Life

A vibrant community where students thrive academically, socially, and personally

Dormitories

Safe, comfortable, and modern living spaces for our boarding students

Home Away From Home

Our modern dormitories provide a safe, supportive environment where students build lifelong friendships and develop independence. With dedicated residential staff and comprehensive support services, we ensure every boarding student feels at home.

Accommodations

2-4 person rooms with private bathrooms, study areas, and storage

Amenities

High-speed WiFi, common rooms,computer lab, and study lounges

Supervision

24/7 residential staff, dorm parents, and security personnel

Activities

Weekend trips, dorm events, and social activities

Dining Services

Nutritious, delicious meals to fuel student success

Healthy Options

Balanced meals with fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. All meals prepared by professional chefs.

Special Diets

Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, options available. We accommodate all dietary needs and preferences.

Dining Hall

Modern cafeteria with comfortable seating for 500+ students.

Entertainment Programs

Every Saturday night, our boarding students enjoy heartwarming and exciting activities that build community and create lasting memories.

Christian Movie Nights

We screen inspirational and faith-based Christian movies that provide both entertainment and moral guidance, fostering a positive spiritual atmosphere among students.

Football Matches

Sports fans get together to cheer on their favorite teams during special football match screenings, bringing the excitement of the pitch right into our campus.
