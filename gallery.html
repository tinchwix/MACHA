Student Activities

At MACHA, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals. Our diverse range of activities helps students discover their passions and develop leadership skills.

  • Student Government & Leadership Council - Developing future leaders
  • Inter-house Sports Competitions - Football, basketball, volleyball & athletics
  • Choir & Music Club - Weekly practices and festival performances
  • Christian Fellowship & Sabbath Programs - Spiritual growth activities
  • Drama & Creative Arts Club - Cultural performances and exhibitions
  • Science & Environmental Club - Experiments and conservation projects
