  • Parent interview

  • Academic transcripts

Essential items every student needs for a successful school year

  10. Sport shoes
  11. Enough soap
  12. Toothpaste and toothbrush
  13. Shoe polish

  15. Cup, plate and spoon (CK brands, Red for girls, Blue for boys)

Uniform (2 Pairs)

Primary
  1. Tunic grey dress

  3. Sky blue shirts

Junior Secondary (JSS)
  1. Navy blue skirt / Navy blue trouser
